An additional constraint

on original Tower of Hanoi Puzzle
Move the tower of discs to a different post.
You may move one ring at a time.

Each move is a lift, then a flip to place.

You may only place smaller rings on top of bigger rings.
AND OPPOSITES ATTRACT, LIKES REPEL,
so you may NOT have two of the same sides of the magnets touching.

What is the fewest number of moves?
